What We Fix

Clogged drains and sewer backups are more than just an inconvenience—they're a warning sign that something is wrong with your plumbing system. Whether it's a slow kitchen sink, a toilet that won't flush, or a complete sewer line backup, Frisco Elite Plumbing identifies the root cause and clears the blockage completely.

We serve homeowners and businesses across Frisco who are tired of temporary fixes. Our team uses professional-grade equipment—hydro-jetting, mechanical augering, and video camera inspections—to remove stubborn clogs, tree roots, and debris that standard plungers can't handle. We don't just clear the immediate blockage; we identify why it happened and recommend solutions to prevent it from happening again.

The problem solved: No more backed-up water, foul odors, or the stress of wondering if your entire plumbing system is about to fail. We restore full drainage flow and give you peace of mind that your system is working properly.

Who It's For

Homeowners with slow drains, businesses with frequent clogs, and property managers dealing with aging plumbing systems. If water is backing up anywhere in your home or business, this service is for you.

The Problem

Clogged drains cause water damage, mold growth, and unpleasant odors. Sewer backups can contaminate your property and create serious health hazards. Delaying service only makes the damage worse and more expensive.

The Solution

Professional drain cleaning with hydro-jetting, augering, and camera inspections to locate blockages, remove roots, and restore full drainage. We work fast and thoroughly so you're back to normal quickly.

How We Clear Your Drains

Our proven process gets water flowing again quickly and safely.

1

Initial Assessment

We listen to your description, ask diagnostic questions, and determine the severity of the clog. If it's an emergency, we prioritize your call.

2

On-Site Inspection

Our technician arrives and inspects the affected drains. We check water flow, look for signs of backup, and test multiple fixtures to map the blockage location.

3

Camera Inspection (if needed)

For main line or recurring clogs, we insert a video camera to see exactly what's blocking the line—roots, collapsed pipe, or debris—and show you the problem.

4

Professional Clearing

We use hydro-jetting, mechanical augering, or both to remove the blockage completely. Hydro-jetting is especially effective for grease, roots, and mineral buildup.

5

Testing & Prevention

We test all fixtures to confirm full drainage, discuss what caused the clog, and recommend preventive maintenance or repairs to avoid future backups.
