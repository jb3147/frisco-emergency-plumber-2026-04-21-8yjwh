Emergency Plumbing Services

Whether it is a sudden leak, a failed water heater, or a backed-up drain in the middle of the night, Frisco Elite Plumbing delivers licensed, 24/7 service to get your home or business back to normal quickly.

Professional Plumbing Services in Frisco

Every service is delivered by licensed technicians, backed by fully stocked service vehicles and around-the-clock emergency response.

Burst Pipe & Major Leak Repair

We quickly locate and isolate burst pipes and major leaks to limit structural damage and water waste. Our team performs durable repairs that stand up to pressure, temperature swings, and long-term use.

Available 24/7 for slab leaks, ceiling leaks, and sudden pipe failures in homes and commercial properties.

Drain, Sewer & Toilet Emergencies

Backed-up drains and overflowing toilets can shut down your home or business in minutes. We clear clogs, restore proper flow, and identify root issues so the problem stays fixed.

From main line backups to recurring slow drains, we use professional-grade equipment to get your system moving again fast.

Water Heater Repair & Replacement

No hot water, leaking tanks, or unreliable temperatures are treated as high priority. We repair most water heater issues on the first visit and provide honest guidance when replacement is the smarter option.

We service traditional and tankless units, ensuring safe operation and efficient performance for your household or business.

Gas Line & Appliance Emergencies

Gas smells, appliance shutoffs, and suspected line damage demand immediate attention. Our licensed technicians safely shut down, test, and repair gas lines to protect your family and property.

We handle emergency diagnostics for gas water heaters, stoves, and other appliances in compliance with Texas safety standards.

Commercial Emergency Plumbing

Restaurants, offices, retail locations, and multi-unit buildings rely on uninterrupted plumbing. We prioritize commercial calls, working quickly to minimize downtime and protect your reputation.

Discreet, professional service with clear communication so owners, managers, and tenants know exactly what is happening at every step.

Preventive Inspections & Peace-of-Mind Checks

After an emergency, we can inspect your entire system to catch weak spots before they fail. Our licensed experts check fixtures, shutoffs, supply lines, and drains for early warning signs.

Ideal for new homeowners, recent renovations, or anyone who wants confidence that their plumbing is ready for Texas weather and heavy use.
